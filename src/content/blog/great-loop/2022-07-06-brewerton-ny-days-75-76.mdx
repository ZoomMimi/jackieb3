The Marina put on a party for the Fourth of July fireworks…on the third. They provided hamburgers, hotdogs and flatware. Everyone brought a dish. The locals and the loopers (there were 5 boats representing AGLCA) mixed very well. We had super views of the fireworks right form our slip.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjBV1EDAmY51Xq4KxpIy9JXRi_d3JPAxZVg2fD0AQWId9wIcqRb12j9k-jqzccEKgdlwhD3F6GqaIdZXB70_eO6vGl9RIHRXtMvVzHb3toZF7uB5jplUDQz1sF9D03OoklNSpE9C_Z_gFRSLFVldhrd0Yg_HwWWlBQhl4ob-MQyWRUj-XA0dK3i73Mi/s864/C3FCCAE2-9AE7-47B7-B70A-1FA78B31F4CF.jpeg)

So we met new Looper friends who actually know some of our New Bern friends. My good friend Christine, who was the librarian at Camelot, the last school I taught in came by for an after noon visit. It was wonderful to reconnect. Her mother lives close by. Note the beautiful view behind Christine and I. I could have stayed here a while longer.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhRj8WaNiWyqbRwS1FSDpttHhBA8Fzel3y4mCJx_a2bSfLRlWWUqEFJ4hz0W3wT02nuYFWzOvah77pvyBh6QHM56FUtfqB2V4O0AkJY8Kb4a-PG4EcxQbkU13cKZc3uIMloJRegCWL3krNbdRBj8BOCxvqY630Dr1g7AAWumafB03-du7lL_UkqwUAH/s4032/C172C787-CAC5-4A25-96D4-8CAA47D877FD.jpeg)
