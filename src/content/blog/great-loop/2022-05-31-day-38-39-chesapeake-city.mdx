Today our mood matches the weather. Mike is trying to track down a generator mechanic AND a generator. The first mechanic he spoke with was not interested in such a big project at their time of the year when there is so much work available just getting boats back into the water for the summer. He also told us that there are major supply issues just getting a generator.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjZmrkcrwMh7Uj6BhrdhIN6Rtu7gxzB7xc7XFXjUR3v3dPMFyLBh-RnzVEwxPoiFFXI_fM9dtKYMUgAG_debOFUNBywYhCAR6T83dtas_dN8kZ_z0UMOdi0yHEdhmsPaFQD98y2v_flslGhtDdEPbLgOeZdUa4oU8vca-7zpnnML999QUtQcj9vtgsI/w558-h419/26580C8D-A75F-43A4-A26B-0AA8B2F40C14.jpeg)

Luckily before we reached Chesapeake City Mike located a mechanic in Cape May and the mechanic was able to locate a generator. Looks like we will be waiting in Cape May for the elusive generator and the installation.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiS19Gw_qG0_f1R_0syaWzz5Y9q10ANZMoSIj_5p3NqhVm2QfywO45fXjZNJ0nHNH6kYVFHPrGY0zRFdJhIP69eSjlffFtHOslEfd70tSfJ9M_4TIDmVqJBNds-SaGIgT4NgM9BzsKmjDgmGTQXEDGVBxC3c8B69JXGFsIYozFctbzlXgW8X5kFbUVo/s320/4B5B87CE-4B76-472B-B116-CF1888F7D63F.jpeg)

In the morning we took a dingy ride across the canal to the historic town of Chesapeake City. We met some "Loopers" and came back in the evening for dinner with a group of eight.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEicvG1_pwn_uVXN-G8IIauUQOhNvdSWDdSV7vioGWlEiZgJ_NO7Wdt4noDRurlNxgcTwrhpNmghKYnLJvK6W1X_5NqOTS0ClfkCX4oPY7b6Iic6UNaOCzvjAu3hthkO_Y9i4LwCRIJ7UL_2WtWejcKSnscKO2fFCaKR3JHtoia380I_pyZ1QCWMkI2u/s320/A705DFE4-BB95-49FA-A412-DC4C60AE7442.jpeg)

We were lucky to see several ships and barges go by while we were weathered in an extra day.
