On our last day we did the trifecta on bikes with our new friends Don and Kay. The Cold Spring Brewery is a non-profit brewery in a state run historical village. The Nauti Distillery uses produce from their farm and has wonderful cocktails. The best discovery was the Turdo Winery. They had excellent small batch wines. We all made it safely back to the marina.

For those who are interested we have a short video of the process of exchanging a generator in a 455 Cruisers Motor Yacht. This is what Mike did while I toured the town. The amazing part of this is that we were able to do this in 3 weeks. We found out from the dealer how "lucky" we were. Most people are waiting nearly a year to get a replacement generator. That would have been a Loop stopper for sure.
