Today we finally ventured out of Deltaville heading to Solomon's Island. It was foggy when first woke up but it looked as if the fog was lifting. I sat up on the dash for the best view of crab pots lurking in the bay.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgLcF8DfK_I2_m7zCtNpVLldDBgdAtGKB4ugV4n9gWvdZJRyOk1NDT8qGNnTD7DuUf0TYzM3UAx0hdXAlVy1z3RIp-e6hywb55xFrbbSBfYaQczmNERHLI4xe_02V7yPwAzsxYK4s4iB_8YL9IAlYBgcKtXOa5kwGrmbM267py6BX9c0tptP3U7cCDN/s320/BE588AFC-60D8-48B7-B39E-D07FA7412E8C.jpeg)

We set out with buddy boat Dog House and entertained them with our auto pilot troubles. Turns out we had accidentally hit the reverse course button and the boat kept trying to make a course correction by turning us around in a big circle.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEj8qvOvsx5PjjtBxIjFazUy1ef0SG6105QOiXIWtZ2U42GXCKSwT5g8KkGhYrm6DWlw5WuqvMjsUximR_RUbyjk0GaSDU2ZEQi8hLS54dAjSc_9oWdH-vFFyeP_J4rQTmwcHGhJ9RVHnl4Wvnz74Db8zIMmAyD6Um9Exif54xlVnQGlNji_2IKpUiBi/s320/68FC3E05-E3A1-4B32-BAD8-848D2F627E1C.jpeg)

First of many lighthouses on the Chesapeake

We did a quick drive around Solomon Island before ending up back at Linda's Sister's house.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEiG69XtT6NqRhzECDrPVL1H67Da7NZd5u5zWm-CfkCWxvgpoHx47NWH838DgHs3qf9YtsDaEjo193B5qi6gAk7oLXzQ_i-8kI3-VA0wo48ewrPgn9sCab8VM3iybVsaZ--wHwKl97-IwSAbDxEIKtTA4lNeN0tWQsYhVLLHQ2EHjvs9ifOFOhnd5alZ/w679-h261/551BC62D-9575-4D18-8C1F-906A9EFAADFB.jpeg)

We were so fortunate to be able to dock at her property. She owns the entire point at green buoy #5 as you come into Solomons, Maryland. It's just spectacular! We did not fit into the boat house but we were able to tie off to the pier and piling for the slip next to the boat house.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhBewJXcpoXHayP19tBbKozqklPl-KHekjjIMQxZAdP3caLvFd7JBwakjudI2kKY-d4qhavR-psfOiQvQQ_c6HWxP27MqdSu9jGKpD8AcrR-Ev_y-XSTqvtOVu_SAH_CXevgg9atw_ZZCMM3S1GvqtRyicM6j4qjPR5TLlICJArggJSdgkt0W39Q8LW/w501-h376/7E39E3F1-EB67-428E-88FC-C2E18052579C.jpeg)

We had a great time with our friends Tom and Linda. We went for a dingy ride and then they took us to Walmart to get oil for the boat. They even spent the night on the boat to try it out since they are considering joint us somewhere on the loop.
