![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgjO5au7c50bmFzurheMNuAzuGQzyst8n4fyA6FGgv9n0eCkRob-8Yo6LXFNGnpr6tJYwx_5yYIEMpEcf1TzAEdX1gc-O1bpL0pKrPOPfXSzc1RSylpdtw8P68whdJCOLgQa1Aedxt_yUUxFs5I8k4JLrnmZu-ofoMBZ1xhOCBqitH69cGf7fjww0hd/w427-h569/C6C3CCF5-AC21-4635-96F9-842D5B4DA867.jpeg)

We were in like sardines. But we made new friends on our way. We ended up traveling like this together for 5 locks. It was a lot of work for me as I had to raise and lower fenders on different sides of the boats as some locks had port (left) side tie ups and others had starboard (right.)

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgpqVpZZ1TWDZALo5AIfo2l1w-ZkOuZKdEqkKpy27ZB_efQk8THC2Jly4BEboF2lUanPWRIj7ww6lT3RioYupKhiBJ7bdzbHxXKssiqrZbBiIPVXrbVa_uiJKG_oLNrL745EaNjq3VfdVXm0aXCL6RYPYjS0IlKzl5_-06Njpbfs93tugiK-1FYzIxi/w527-h395/192BD1D9-F771-4F43-9868-70B977906794.jpeg)

Being the larger boat we always had the wall and the other boat rafted to us. These two rode along with us at every lock but one. (Yay , new friends!)

Of course the scenery along the way was Gorgeous.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjvS-URvq5m8v-1YH8GC9eWKejssXker1rMHvx2f8xjcvrYriOteS9LWiyHs98VNqqDqPxF89Td8WsmYvVoI_tG6eTwjv7-0XiFOKmMgmnB8cSCqGAQTAZDc8c4m2o_gZsK6mfRh6il4KMtKyik3R6xLhz_Xan2HlPY-lLZBs0GUk9bhzzYwb4IA9CB/w542-h407/D17FA355-CCFF-4BF2-B9C7-898E0C99C53D.jpeg)

After an extremely long day with so much locking, we all went to the local watering hole for happy hour and ended up back on our boat for movie night. We even had overnight guests as one of the boats with us is a center console with a bow tent and we had thunder storms over night. One of the best days so far.
