Pickwick Dam has two chambers. We entered the smaller chamber with two other boats. We had a big lift ahead of us.

As we were being lifted a barge entered the chamber next to us going down stream.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh4x4UTUJlw6P77ZEtUElfE59X5TNRuz34Zc9-_RVY033R2JBTXm2lg6aUXgknrde79_5yOWLm5z3JQCk_yqjjTNIpkEapEKbktkEEvKrPF0AdXucMZ1-CMFc1uLZMDjo_XAjQ1CFkvV6uP_xJKkcwyxuMEc3u6pa7i_4hyphenhyphengL5rLdPqxUU6LCpakd1isis/w583-h213/A22F9DCD-D02C-498D-8CA3-0B093E138CB9.jpeg)
