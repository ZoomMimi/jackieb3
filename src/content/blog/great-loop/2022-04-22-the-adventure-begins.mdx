Our first stop is at Belhaven, NC. We are staying at the River Forest Manor and Marina; a top notch facility with golf carts available to visit this cute little town. We have a great sunset view and tomorrow's sunrise should also be spectacular as our boat is on a pier facing south and no boats on either side of us. We did meet several other looper boats today thanks to the Nebo app. It was a great Day One!
