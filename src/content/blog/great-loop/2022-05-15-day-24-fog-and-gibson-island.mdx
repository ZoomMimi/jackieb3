Today we woke to more fog. It was supposed to lift by 8:00.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgRzFD2TqRsJsVIoKvvFI5RZufH3HqKF0ck3ITTkVnp_DFgngN2TewgA5Ao03kKy0ma7P8ZVwHEu0CzB6m08NXKc3gDpTzC9XDRyMYsn94vFviqbtJgXVjY8rJbbxfuaJLFFqgC-sy6ije5dZs5CHIwZSRAThxTbcfGFjMfg_i7Rppv_2gjgInAyWS5/s4032/F7A645EB-C7FE-430D-8B84-9FEA998087EA.jpeg)

It didn't. Barbara kept watch out on the bow as we moved out very slowly, confident the fog would lift.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjs7WRWhS_MOgNeVAiwKMxR4zR9iSFJkOJ7_zV4n1P1GCcB0zVztl7ruO3AgxL3wdq7dDSkfj58b_-_xQIYtlKWpXPh8Iy-SEeuPPUifEGuvsaGdmzOcEDVeD-FxGsi8pbNGxpOnhtteZ2h26c7yr1TqyU4KkRNhXTc7jWAU9oJAurWVH_G1BPOU4Bt/s4032/D8148A1C-AF40-4D47-903C-46283F1980C1.jpeg)

It did not. Barbara moved her watch inside as it was really cold and wet out there and we had very few crab pot sightings.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjvxhO1hxS0EtfvxWmLgKtwTqd3YwSZhZAS528u7dwZFEzLFivG8lK6IlsCVwK3apJDd3N7bqgzLXI65kgCuwNvYnY7uJ1lKwBd5jLUUW_7R0z4eXjG4NWIBEc88doG_zDecbI7KTJBFDu2uT1xsvJ1AKUHU14XzU1ODK4LnhABCrpNxE2so-yx3VJt/s4032/51AF9C44-9387-498E-9F02-6B7623E97E46.jpeg)

But then we saw this pop out of the fog at about 18 knots. Very thankful for all the electronics we have on the boat. Very spooky thinking about how the early settlers did this.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhgk8nsgpv_nqZtt_Jc9BEF3M-k1j6HKvreQM4SMar5_b_ghtyAmAwR_42clVRdkFgAyltXvNMbp-5z1dan1yUDYQld8e0z5ih5eCaVwgH-_AmnMhNrEZ9G2Z4f2-TqF2fv5nq-s3JtYqKkRR1YkgZ5eQYupaM0_1I09ZV6YFF6_1-vtYNJezu5of-B/s960/BF18D1C7-3693-40F6-8DDC-E8E675BB8F05.jpeg)

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjqNr3YryvmVqqXeSIt5NU28pmL74Jankx2oaKHRaPVw5-qMYLmNR-lH7T5F4qd4nhXcP0G5Nu1dSbZN2H7YNj9wTsaL_K0zcFN9PmQhT5OZFbZgw_FYvk37JC1hwhmYPdiq4DjCcDTEo2froIPJzgCjXeNi7MXctp6Ch4p-jbbTTMak8Sajf6NMKPh/s4032/A3E0F6ED-5BB9-4F10-99AD-740F6A91A7FE.jpeg)
