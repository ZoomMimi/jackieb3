The past couple of weeks have seen a flurry of activity as we seriously get ready for our big looping journey. By 'we" I mean Mike. He really can do it all. He installed a hardwood floor over the winter, but this week he has put to use an amazing number of skills. He wired a new back up camera, fixed the hot water heater (this one wasn't on the to do list but went straight to the top), repaired the salon air conditioner by replacing the main circuit board and a whole host of preventative maintenance that I won't even attempt to list here. We are taking one last trip to visit Lindsay's family for Easter leaving our car and trailer/garage with her. We leave in 6 days😳
