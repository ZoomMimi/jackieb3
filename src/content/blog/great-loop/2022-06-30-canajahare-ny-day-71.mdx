There was another looper at the dock in Canajahare. Carmine from the vessel Calm Seas. He told us all the restaurants in town were closed because it was Tuesday so we had hike over for dinner. I love collecting new friends.

After dinner we tried to find some ice cream. Mike ended up walking to McDonalds. The fire boat show was amazing.
