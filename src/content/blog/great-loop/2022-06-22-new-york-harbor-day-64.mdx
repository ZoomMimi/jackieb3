We were fortunate to travel through the harbor with "looper" friends on Charley Girl that we met in Cape May. This was not as simple as it sounds. When we went to pull our anchor in the morning NOTHING (electronics) worked. Mike was able to get things working again and Mike and Gina were kind enough to wait for us. It only took about an hour. Thanks for taking our pictures!
