Today we passed through our first lock and the amazing Norfolk Naval Shipyard on our way to Lynnhaven Inlet and Broad Bay, which is on the north side of Virginia Beach.  We made safely to our friends pier up Broad Creek where we'll be staying until Sunday, May 1.  We'll get some maintenance done here and catch up with several old friends.  Next we'll head back to Norfolk for the Looper's Spring Rendezvous from 2-5 May.  Stay tuned.
