<objective>
Write `scripts/04-generate-stubs.mjs` — the final output stage of the data pipeline. It
consumes the already-built enriched voyage timeline (product of upstream scripts 00–03,
covering DATA-01/02/03/04/06) and produces two outputs:

1. **Backfill (Job 1, DATA-05):** Add `miles`/`hours` to existing MDX posts that have
   matching Nebo data, and `lat`/`lon` to the 4 posts still missing them. Edits in-place.

   undocumented day with 10+ photos in the April 2022 – May 2024 range, including the full
   last segment through 2024-05-17. Each stub carries full frontmatter, `VoyageStats`, and
   `Gallery`.

Upstream pipeline outputs already exist (STATE.md pipeline table: scripts 00–03 WORKING).
This plan does NOT re-run GPX/EXIF/email extraction — it reads their merged product,
`voyage-timeline-enriched.json`, and verifies its integrity before consuming it.

Purpose: Turn pipeline data into site content — the bridge from raw voyage data to
publishable posts (completed by Barbara in Phase 6).
Output: One script + ~45 enriched posts + ~253 draft stubs.
</objective>
